⚠️ What is Pharming?

Pharming is a cyber attack where hackers redirect users to a fake website that looks like a real banking site.

They trick users into entering login details like username, password, or OTP.

⚠️ Always double-check the website URL before logging in.

⚠️ Example Scenario

You click a link from a message → it opens a fake bank site → you enter your login → attackers steal your account.

Stay alert. Stay safe.
